📥 SE Leaderboard Exporter

Bypass the UI restrictions and download your complete StreamElements leaderboard as a CSV file instantly.

🔍 How to find your Channel ID & JWT Token ▼
  1. Log into your StreamElements Dashboard.
  2. Click on your profile avatar in the top right corner and select Channel settings.
  3. Under the Channel info tab, you will see your Account ID (this is your Channel ID).
  4. Right next to or below it, is the JWT Token.
  5. Copy both values and paste them into the form fields below.
  6. The two squares next to the JWT Token can be used to copy it without showing your secret.
🛡️ Privacy & Security Guarantee This is a free, open-source utility. Your JWT Token acts as the master key to your account. We NEVER save, log, or store your token. It is held in temporary server memory for the seconds it takes to generate your file, and is immediately destroyed.
